This website is a rough compilation of things I've gathered over the years - many of them used to live in my Notes app or photo gallery, but I've decided to assemble them all here. You'll find [articles I've enjoyed reading](https://aaronaftab.com/lists/articles), [my favorite songs](https://aaronaftab.com/music), [snapshots from my camera roll](https://aaronaftab.com/pictures), and more. I also like writing, so I'll be publishing the occasional piece/story/incoherent rambling [here](https://aaronaftab.com/writing).

Here's a bit more about me - 

- I listen to a *lot* of music (racked up over 100,000 minutes on [<span style="color: yellowgreen">Spotify</span>](https://open.spotify.com/user/6u90cbdam5c6wpaux1g2zql5v) in 2020) - my favorite at the moment is indie pop, but I listen to everything from hip-hop to country to EDM.
- I love traveling - I've been to most of the major cities in the US (top two are New York and Seattle), a ton of national parks ([<span style="color: slateblue">Glacier National Park</span>](https://www.visitmt.com/places-to-go/glacier-national-park) is my favorite), and a handful of other countries (including Norway!). 
- I'm a big sports fan, mainly football, soccer, and basketball. My favorite players are [<span style="color: darkgoldenrod">Alvin Kamara</span>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=JGFeqc6s20g), [<span style="color: firebrick">Kevin de Bruyne</span>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y4-JCBwQu9k), and [<span style="color: darkgreen">Giannis Antetokounmpo</span>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GLBQNOd63x8). 
- I like spending time outside - hiking, swimming, playing [<span style="color: gold">Spikeball</span>](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CmOiaelqExQ), etc. I swam competitively for about ten years growing up, which I blame for my occasional clumsiness on land and inability to throw a perfect spiral.
- I read a lot, both fiction and nonfiction - tons of YA books when I was younger, now anything I get my hands on. Some of my favorites:

    - [<span style="color: darkorange">The Hammock</span>](https://poets.org/poem/hammock), a poem by Li-Young Lee
