About Me

A bit more about my background, current focus, and what drives me.

I graduated from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Statistics. My career has been an exciting journey focusing on Product Management, especially in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), backed by a solid foundation in Software Engineering.

Currently, I'm channeling my energy into Mirage AI, a venture I founded to explore innovative image stylization solutions. I also take on independent consulting projects through Battery Park Advisors. Before this, I had the opportunity to contribute to some great teams at Capital One, Bilt Rewards, Rize Education, and through internships at Microsoft, Alpaca, and DocuSign.

Beyond my professional life, I'm deeply engaged with technology, enjoy methodical personal finance and travel planning, have a keen appreciation for global cultures, and am always committed to continuous learning. This profile reflects my journey and experiences as of May 2025.
